Under the Dome

Chai's Review: Under the Dome

This subtitle translation project aims to enable non-Chinese audiences to learn about the air quality conditions China is facing, throught the investigation brought by Chai Jing, a Chinese television celebrity.

Contributors

Thanks to the volunteers who participate in this project. Their names are listed on GitHub /subtitles/CONTRIBUTORS.md. Special thanks to Miss Chai Jing.

Organizers

Linghein Ho (@tichaing) and Tianyu Fang (@tianyuf), high school / junior high school students in mainland China.

Multilingualism

We encourage subtitles in other languages to be contributed. If you need any help on the translation or technical support, feel free to fork us on GitHub or contact us via email.

Properties

This project is voluntary and non-profit. The copyrights of translated subtitles belong to multiple holders who contributed in the project. The documentary and its contents obey the license from its copyright holders.

Report

This project has been proofread by native English speakers, but please inform us when you have discovered any incorrect parts or mistakes.
